Easy Delicious Protein Smoothie Recipe!

Welcome! Get ready to make the Tastiest Breakfast Smoothie of You're life!

Ingredients

  • 600ml Filtered Water or Milk (Your Choice!)
  • 1 Bannana Chopped
  • 100g Frozen Strawberries
  • 100g Frozen Blueberris
  • 100g Frozen Mango Chunks
  • 1 Scoop Protein Powder of Your Choice

Instructions

  1. Pour purified water or milk into blender
  2. Add scoop of protein powder
  3. Add Stawberries
  4. Add Bannana
  5. Add Mango chunks
  6. Add Blueberries
  7. Blend on high untill smooth consitancy
  8. Switch off blender and serve into tall Glass
  9. Last but not least Enjoy!
